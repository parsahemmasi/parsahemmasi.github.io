About Me

Passionate about robotics and technology, I bring a diverse range of research interests, including Machine Learning, Robotics, AI, Autonomous Vehicles, Computer Vision, Internet of Things, and Tiny ML on Edge Devices, shaping my professional journey. My educational background comprises a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from Shahid Beheshti University and a Diploma in Mathematics and Physics from Danesh High School. Notably, I've earned accolades like the Iran's National Elites Foundation (INEF) Fellowship and 1st Place in international robotics competitions. As a Head Teaching Assistant and Workshop Lecturer, I've contributed to the academic growth of students, covering topics like FPGA, Microprocessors, VHDL Programming, Digital Circuits Design, Computer Programming, Internet of Things and Machine Learning. My research experiences encompass various projects, one of which involves the field of Autonomous Vehicles while integrating multiple skills such as machine learning, deep learning, and computer vision, among others. This versatile skill set empowers me to thrive in diverse technical domains and contribute to innovation at the intersection of technology and research.
